We only collect the essential information required to automate your operations. This includes, but is not limited to: shift logs, schedule parameters, vendor invoice scans, and communication routing numbers.

We never sell, rent, or distribute your operational data to third parties, competitors, or marketing agencies. Your venue's performance metrics are strictly confidential and encrypted at rest.

All voice memos, text logs, and employee rosters are encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We utilize robust access controls, ensuring only your authorized personnel can view sensitive outputs.
